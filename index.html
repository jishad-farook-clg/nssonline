About NSS Management App

What started as NSS FC for Farook College has evolved into something much bigger. Our NSS Management App was born from real-world experience managing NSS activities at the college level.

After successfully streamlining NSS operations at Farook College, we realized the potential to help NSS units across all colleges. The new NSS Management App builds upon our proven foundation, incorporating feedback from real users and adding powerful new features designed for multi-college environments.

This isn't just an upgrade – it's a complete reimagining of how NSS activities can be managed, tracked, and coordinated across educational institutions.
